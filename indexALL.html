H1-B Petition

What is the H-1B Visa?

H-1B visa is used by businesses and organizations in the United States to employ foreign nationals with the preferred qualifications, knowledge, and expertise in a role. The petition is filed with USCIS, along with the requisite fees.

What are the Legal Requirements for Applying for the H-1B Visa?

The application process for an H-1B visa is complex and lengthy. There are legal requirements on the employee and employer sides. To begin the process, the applicant must first find a sponsor. That sponsor must submit a Labor Conditions Approval and Form I-129. Then, the employer will work with the sponsored employee to complete an H1B visa petition.

Any US employer can sponsor a H1-B petition, however, it looks like big accounting/consulting firms and tech companies have higher numbers of the applications approved in past 6 years. In 2017, Amazon and Google were not in top 10. However, in 2022, Amazon moved up to Top 1 and Google moved up to Top3.
